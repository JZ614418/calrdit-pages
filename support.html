Common questions

Do I need an account?
No. Everything lives on your device. There's no sign-up, no server database.

What does "Calrdit Cloud" mean?
When you don't paste your own OpenAI key, the app sends the food-recognition request through our Cloudflare Worker, which forwards it to OpenAI. We don't keep the request content — only a per-device counter for the daily quota. See the privacy policy for the exact wording.

Can I use my own OpenAI key?
Yes. Settings → OpenAI API Key → paste your key (starts with sk-). Requests will then go straight from your phone to OpenAI; our servers never see them. Your key is stored only in your device Keychain.

Will my data sync across devices?
Yes, via your private iCloud database (CloudKit). Same Apple ID → same records. We have no visibility into this sync.

How do I reset everything?
Settings → Start over → Reset all data. This clears your profile and every logged transaction on this device. iCloud sync will propagate the reset to your other devices.

I forgot to log a meal. Can I backfill?
Yes. Tap + in Wallet → "I forgot a meal" / 补一笔 — pick the time and enter kcal manually.

The AI guessed my meal wrong.
After confirming the payment you can tap the transaction in Activity to edit kcal or delete it. The AI is a helpful estimate, not an oracle — you're the one spending.
